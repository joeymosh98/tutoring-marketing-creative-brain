## Facebook Group Strategy

### Identity

**Name:** Step Up for Students Resources — Florida Families

**Description:** "A community for Florida families using Step Up for Students scholarships. Share resources, ask questions, and get expert guidance on Unique Abilities, PEP, and Family Empowerment scholarships. Managed by education specialists. No spam, no sales pitches — just families helping families."

**Role:** Community resource for ALL Step Up families. Tutero is host and expert contributor, NOT promoter. Brand benefits through association and trust, not selling.

### The Unbreakable Rule

Never sell in the group. When families ask for tutoring recommendations, other families will mention Tutero. That is the organic lead generation mechanism. If Tutero is tagged, respond helpfully and offer to take conversation to DMs. Never post ads, promotional content, or CTAs for Tutero services inside the group.

### Growth to 1,000 Members

- Seed with existing Tutero families, encourage invitations
- Meta ad promoting the group (not Tutero): "Just got your Step Up scholarship? Join 500+ Florida families sharing tips, resources, and support."
- Cross-promote group in every piece of Tutero content
- Lauren, Sarah, and Dani invite from personal networks
- Post in adjacent Facebook groups with genuinely helpful content
- Targets: 100 members month 1, 300 month 3, 1,000 month 6

### Weekly Content Rhythm

- **Monday:** Scholarship tip or EMA portal walkthrough
- **Wednesday:** Community question or poll
- **Friday:** Resource post (link, guide, or tip from the team)
- **Bi-weekly:** Longer educational piece — video from Lauren on IEP challenge, or step-by-step scholarship guide
- **Monthly:** Family story (with permission) illustrating impact of using scholarship funding well
