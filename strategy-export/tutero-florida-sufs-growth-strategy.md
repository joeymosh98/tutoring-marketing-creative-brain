## Critical Context

- This is **Tutero.com** — the 1-on-1 tutoring business for families. NOT tutero.ai (schools product).
- Revenue comes from Florida Step Up for Students scholarships: Unique Abilities (~$10K/yr) and PEP (~$8K/yr).
- Tutero is an approved Step Up provider. Families pay nothing out of pocket.
- The strategic ambition: become the #1 online teaching provider for Step Up families in Florida within 18 months.
- The AI technology powers the lessons but is NOT the brand story. Lead with the human — teachers, account managers, relationships.

## Brand Positioning

### Core Positioning Statement

Tutero is Florida's dedicated teaching practice for students who need more than a classroom can give them. Every Tutero teacher is a qualified, certified educator — not a college student, not a freelance tutor. Every family is guided by a special education specialist who understands IEPs, learning differences, and the emotional weight parents carry. Every lesson is built around the individual student, using proprietary technology that adapts in real time to how they learn. Tutero exists for families who have been let down by systems that were supposed to help — and who deserve a partner that will walk alongside them for years.

### Category: Dedicated Teaching

Tutero does not compete in "online tutoring." It owns the category of **Dedicated Teaching** — long-term, personalized, 1-on-1 education delivered by qualified teachers who know your child by name, supported by specialists who understand your family's journey.

### The "Only We" Statement

Only Tutero combines qualified, certified teachers with special education specialist account managers, AI-personalized lessons built for each student, and a long-term partnership model — all accessible through Florida's Step Up for Students scholarship at no out-of-pocket cost to the family.

| Context | Use This | Never This |
|---|---|---|
| The people teaching | Teachers | Tutors |
| What they deliver | Lessons | Sessions / hours |
| The company | A teaching practice | A platform / service |
| Students served | Students with unique abilities | Special needs kids |
| What families get | Dedicated teaching | Online tutoring |
| Scholarship framing | Scholarship-funded | Free (except in ad headlines) |
| Account managers | Education specialists | Sales reps / customer service |
| The technology | Personalized lesson technology | AI-powered anything |

### Scholarship Language Hierarchy

- **In paid ads (scroll-stopping):** "Free" works. "Free 1-on-1 teaching for Florida families with Step Up scholarships."
- **On landing pages:** "Scholarship-funded." "Your Step Up scholarship covers every lesson — there's no out-of-pocket cost."
- **In discovery calls:** Drop both. "The Step Up scholarship covers Tutero completely, so we can focus on finding the right teacher for [child's name]."
- **In brand/community content:** Never "free." Position the scholarship as an entitlement families earned.

## Brand Voice

The voice of the school principal who actually knows every student's name. Warm but not saccharine. Knowledgeable but never condescending. Direct but never salesy.

### Voice Principles

1. **Lead with empathy, follow with expertise.** Acknowledge the parent's reality before presenting Tutero's solution.
2. **Be specific, never vague.** "Every Tutero teacher holds a teaching certification and has classroom experience" > "Our teachers are qualified."
3. **Respect intelligence.** These parents understand IEPs, accommodations, and educational law. Meet them where they are.
4. **Honor vulnerability without exploiting it.** Never use a child's struggle as a hook without showing the path forward.

## Audience Segments

### Segment 1: Parents of Children with IEPs / Unique Abilities

The most informed, most exhausted, most skeptical audience. Lead with acknowledgment of the parent's experience navigating systems.

**Primary message:** "You've fought hard for your child. Tutero is the partner who fights alongside you — with real teachers who understand learning differences and education specialists who've spent their careers in special education."

### Segment 2: Homeschool / PEP Families

Already bold, already alternative. Need practical help: a qualified teacher for subjects they can't teach, flexible scheduling, and confidence their PEP scholarship covers it.

**Primary message:** "You chose homeschooling because your child deserves better. Tutero gives your child a qualified teacher for the subjects you want expert help with — and your PEP scholarship covers every lesson."

### Segment 3: Newly Approved Scholarship Families

Overwhelmed by EMA portal, confused by provider lists, anxious about choosing wrong. Most educatable and most likely to become fiercely loyal.

**Primary message:** "Just received your Step Up scholarship? We'll walk you through exactly how to use it — from the EMA portal to finding the right teacher for your child."

### Segment 4: Parents Whose Child Is Struggling in School

At a turning point. Child is failing or disengaged. Many don't yet know about Step Up.

**Primary message:** "If your child is struggling in school, it's not because they can't learn — it's because they haven't found the right teacher yet."
