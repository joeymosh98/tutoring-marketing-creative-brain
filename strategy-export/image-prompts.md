# Nano Banana 2 — Image Prompts

Generate each image, save to this folder with the filename shown. All images are 1080×1350 (4:5) for Instagram feed.

**Shared style (apply to every prompt):** Shot on iPhone in natural light, candid and unposed, slight grain, soft warm colour grade (cream/amber cast), lightly edited in VSCO, not a marketing photo. No eye contact with camera. No big open-mouthed smiles. No studio lighting. No stock-photo styling. No matching outfits. No perfect compositions. Slight imperfections welcomed — a water bottle out of place, a crumpled page, soft skin texture.

**Consistent character (where a child appears):** Australian girl, about 13 years old, shoulder-length brown hair often tied up loosely, freckles across the nose, wearing a plain hoodie or t-shirt. Keep her consistent across images so the carousels feel like one family's story. For the parent, a mum, early 40s, soft features, no makeup, wearing a cardigan or jumper.
