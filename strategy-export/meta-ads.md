# Tutero Florida — Meta Ads Strategy

## Creative Mix Ratio

40% direct-response (pain points + scholarships) / 30% social proof + testimonials / 20% educational + scholarship awareness / 10% brand storytelling

---

## Ten Ad Concepts

### Ad 1: The IEP Parent (Video — Direct Response)
**Headline:** "Your child's IEP goals deserve a real teacher."
**Body:** "If you're tired of tutors who don't understand your child's IEP, Tutero is different. Every teacher is qualified and certified. Your education specialist has spent her career in special education. And your Florida Unique Abilities Scholarship covers every lesson. No cost to your family. Meet the team that finally gets it."
**Creative:** UGC-style talking head. Lauren or Sarah to camera, warmly and confidently. Living room/home office. Subtitles essential. 30–45 seconds.

### Ad 2: The Diagnosis Moment (Static — Empathy Lead)
**Headline:** "After the diagnosis, you searched for help. We're here."
**Body:** "When your child was diagnosed, nobody handed you a roadmap. You researched, you advocated, you tried everything. Tutero exists for families like yours — with qualified teachers who understand learning differences and specialists who've spent decades in special education. Your Unique Abilities Scholarship covers everything."
**Creative:** Warm, muted color palette. Parent and child together. Text overlay with headline. No heavy branding.

### Ad 3: The Homework Battle (Video — UA Families)
**Headline:** "What if homework wasn't a battle?"
**Body:** "Every night, same fight. But what if your child had a teacher who understood exactly how they learn — and built every lesson around it? Tutero's qualified teachers use personalized lesson technology so your child is never given work that's too hard, too easy, or too generic. Scholarship-funded. Real teachers."
**Creative:** Video mashup: frustrated homework scene → calm, engaged lesson scene. Text overlay. Music shift tense → warm. 15–20 seconds.

### Ad 4: The Homeschool Math Problem (Static — PEP Families)
**Headline:** "Homeschooling is amazing. Teaching math might not be."
**Body:** "You're an incredible homeschool parent. But not every subject is your strength — and that's okay. Tutero gives your child a qualified teacher for the subjects where you want expert help. Your PEP scholarship covers every lesson. Flexible scheduling that fits your family."
**Creative:** Bright, clean. Parent at table with curriculum materials, slightly overwhelmed but with humor. Light, relatable.

### Ad 5: The Scholarship Explainer (Carousel — Educational)
**Headline:** "Did you know your Florida scholarship can fund 1-on-1 teaching?"
**Body:** Slide 1: Your Step Up scholarship is worth up to $10,000/year. Slide 2: It can fund dedicated 1-on-1 lessons with a qualified teacher. Slide 3: Tutero is an approved provider — our team handles the paperwork. Slide 4: Your child gets a real teacher. You get peace of mind. Start today.
**Creative:** Carousel. Bold text each slide. Branded but not heavy. One clear message per slide.

### Ad 6: The Parent Testimonial (Video — Social Proof)
**Headline:** "'Tutero gave me my son back.' — Keisha, Jacksonville"
**Body:** "Hear from real Tutero families about what changed when their child started working with a dedicated teacher. It's not just grades. It's confidence. It's self-belief. It's the end of the nightly homework battle."
**Creative:** Authentic parent testimonial. Phone-shot or minimal production. Raw, emotional, real. Subtitles essential. 45–60 seconds. Multiple versions for testing.

### Ad 7: The Google Review Mashup (Static — Social Proof)
**Headline:** "This is what families say about Tutero."
**Body:** Compilation of 4–5 real Google review excerpts. Star ratings visible. CTA: "See why families choose Tutero. Scholarship-funded 1-on-1 teaching."
**Creative:** Clean white background. Review text in large font. Star ratings in gold. Minimal branding.

### Ad 8: The Scholarship Deadline (Static — Urgency)
**Headline:** "Step Up scholarship enrollment is open. Don't wait."
**Body:** "Florida families: your child may qualify for up to $10,000/year in scholarship funding for 1-on-1 teaching with a qualified teacher. Tutero is an approved Step Up provider. Check your eligibility today — enrollment periods are limited."
**Creative:** Urgency-driven. Bold headline. Calendar or countdown visual. Run during application windows.

### Ad 9: Lauren's Story (Video — Brand)
**Headline:** "Meet the former school principal guiding every Tutero family."
**Body:** "Lauren spent years as a school principal watching students fall through the cracks of a system that could not serve them individually. Now she leads Tutero's family support team — personally guiding every family, from scholarship paperwork to finding the right teacher. This is why Tutero is different."
**Creative:** Lauren to camera. Professional but warm. Intercut with lesson/family images. 60 seconds.

### Ad 10: The Brand Anthem (Video — Brand Storytelling)
**Headline:** "Every child deserves a teacher who sees them."
**Body:** "Some children do not fit the mold. They learn differently. They need more time, more patience, more creativity. They need a teacher who sees them — not their diagnosis, not their grade level, not their behavior report. Just them. That is what Tutero does. Qualified teachers. Personalized lessons. A team that walks alongside your family for years. Scholarship-funded. No cost to you."
**Creative:** Cinematic. Diverse Florida families montage. Voiceover from parent or Lauren. Emotive music, quiet → hopeful. 60–90 seconds. Use for brand awareness and retargeting.

## Targeting Strategy

**Tier 1 — High Intent:** Interest stacking: IEP advocacy groups, Step Up for Students, homeschool curriculum brands, learning disability awareness orgs, Florida parenting groups. Layer with: parents with K–12 children, Florida geography, education content engagement.

**Tier 2 — Lookalike:** Built from enrolled families and high-quality leads. Start 1%, expand to 3% as source grows. Refresh monthly.

**Tier 3 — Retargeting:** Website visitors who didn't convert, 50%+ video viewers, Facebook group members. Serve testimonial and social proof creative.

---

## Funnel Architecture

1. **Ad impression** → earns the click
2. **Segment-specific landing page** → must mirror the ad's emotional tone. Build dedicated pages for: UA families, PEP/homeschool families, "New to Step Up" families.
3. **Lead capture** → minimal: parent name, child's first name, year level, phone number. Feel like the start of a conversation.
4. **Discovery call** → Lauren, Sarah, or Dani. This is where the relationship begins.

**Critical:** Never send all traffic to a single generic page. Homeschool parents and IEP parents have fundamentally different needs.

---

## CAC Reduction Playbook

Based on Australian campaign learnings:
- Video mashup content and internal team testimonial videos produce lowest CAC
- UGC-style and authentic content outperforms polished creative
- Ads leading with specific pain points outperform generic brand ads
- Static with social proof (Google reviews, smiling faces, parent testimonials) converts at moderate CAC
- Always test 2–3 creative variants per campaign, then reallocate to winners
